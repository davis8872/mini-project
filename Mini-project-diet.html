Intermittent Fasting

Eating during a 12-hour window every day (between 7 a.m. and 7 p.m., for example) and fasting for the other 12 hours. Eating during an 8-hour window every day (between 11 a.m. and 7 p.m., for example) and fasting for the other 16 hours.

  • Fruits,vegetables
  • Brown rice,oats,barley
  • Poultry,fish,beans
Ketogenic Diet

“Ketogenic” is a term for a low-carb diet (like the Atkins diet). The idea is for you to get more calories from protein and fat and less from carbohydrates. You cut back most on the carbs that are easy to digest, like sugar, soda, pastries, and white bread.

  • Fish,seafood
  • Nuts,seeds,healthful oils
  • Low-carb veggies
Mediterranean Diet

The Mediterranean Diet emphasizes plant-based foods and healthy fats. You eat mostly veggies, fruits and whole grains. Olive oil is the main source of fat. Research shows the Mediterranean Diet can lower your risk of cardiovascular disease etc.

  • Vegetables,fruit,nuts
  • Legumes
  • Meats,fish
